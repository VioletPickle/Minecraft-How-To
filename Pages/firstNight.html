How To Survive Your First Night

Step 1:

Find a few trees and cut them down by punching them. Collect all the items that drop. If you get any saplings, plant them.
Note: If you see any sheep, kill them. Sheep drop wool which allows you to make beds.

Step 2:

Make around twenty-eight planks in the crafting menu. Then, make a crafting table puting one plank in each of the crafting slots. Place your crafting table and make eight sticks.

Step 3:

Make a pickaxe, an axe, and a shovel. Break your crafting table and pick it up. Start looking for coal and a good place for your base.
Note: A good spot for your home is a nice flat spot or a larger hill, depending on if you want an underground home or an above-ground one.

Step 4:

When you've found a good place for your base, start your house. A good size to start is 7x7 blocks. Leave an empty spot for a door. Place your crafting table in the house, and make a chest with eight planks. Put your chest down, and drop off some items.

Step 5:

Use your pickaxe and get around twenty stone, then make a furnace. Make a door, too, and place it in the doorway.

Step 6:

If you found coal, make some torches. If you didn't, put two logs in as fuel for every three planks to make charcoal. Then, use the charcoal to make torches. Place a few torches around and inside your house.

Step 7:

If there are trees near your home, cut them down, and if you killed three sheep, make planks if needed and make a bed.

Step 8:

If it is not yet night, go looking for sheep. If it is night, start a mine.
