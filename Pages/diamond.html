How to Make a Diamond Mine

Step 1:

Start mining stairs downwards. If you already have a mine, start from there. Dig down to Y level -58, and then stop. Note: Y level is the middle coordinate.

Step 2:

Mine a two wide hallway to the left of your stairs, and stop after around twenty-five blocks. Go back to your stairs.

Step 3:

Mine a tunnel for a while straight away from your stairs. Make it fairly long. When you decide to finish it, mine another two wide hallway to the left for at least six blocks.

Step 4:

Go back to your one wide tunnel and count three blocks down your two wide hallway. Start mining. There should be two blocks between the two tunnels. Keep mining your tunnel until you get back to the other end. Continue mining tunnels two blocks apart until you have all the diamonds you want. You can always come back later and get more.
